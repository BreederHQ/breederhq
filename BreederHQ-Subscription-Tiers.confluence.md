## ü§ù Referral/Affiliate Program (All Tiers)

### How It Works:

1. **Every paying subscriber gets a referral code**
   * Example: `GOLDENGATELABS` or `BREEDER-AG8F3`

2. **Referrer (existing customer) shares code**
   * "Use my code GOLDENGATELABS for 20% off your first 3 months!"

3. **Referee (new customer) signs up with code**
   * Gets 20% discount on Pro plan: $39 ‚Üí $31.20/month for 3 months
   * Saves $23.40 total

4. **Referrer gets reward**
   * Option A: $20 credit (1 month ~half off)
   * Option B: 20% off their next month
   * Option C: $20 cash via PayPal (if they request payout)

### Limits:
* No cap on referrals per user
* Credits stack (refer 5 users = $100 credit = 2.5 months free)
* Credits apply automatically to next invoice

### Referral Dashboard:
```
- Total referrals: 8
- Active referrals (still paying): 6
- Lifetime earnings: $120 in credits
- Available balance: $40
```

### Why This Works:
* Breeders know other breeders (tight community)
* Word-of-mouth is strongest marketing for this market
* 20% discount is significant ($23 savings) but doesn't kill margins
* Your cost: $11.70 per referee (your share of discount) + $20 referrer credit = $31.70 customer acquisition cost
* If customer stays 12+ months = $468 LTV vs $32 CAC = **14.6x ROI** üöÄ

## üö¶ Quota Enforcement Behaviors

### At Limit (50/50 animals):

**UI:** "Add Animal" button shows badge: "50/50 - Upgrade"

**Click:** Modal appears
* ‚Üí "You've reached your animal limit"
* ‚Üí Show current usage bar (50/50)
* ‚Üí **Options:**
  * [Upgrade to Enterprise - $99/mo] (primary CTA)
  * [Buy +10 Slots - $5/mo] (secondary)
  * [Archive an animal] (tertiary)

### Approaching Limit (45/50 animals):

**UI:** Banner at top of Animals page
* ‚Üí "You're using 45 of 50 animals. Upgrade to add unlimited animals."
* ‚Üí [Upgrade] button
* ‚Üí [Dismiss] (can dismiss, shows again at 48/50)

### Over Limit During Trial ‚Üí Paid Conversion:

**Scenario:** User adds 60 animals during 14-day trial

Trial ends ‚Üí Subscription converts to Pro

System detects: 60 animals > 50 limit

**Options:**

**Option 1 (RECOMMENDED):** Grace period: 7 days to either:
* Archive 10 animals
* Upgrade to Enterprise
* Buy +10 slots add-on

**Option 2:** Hard cutoff: Cannot add more animals until compliant
* Can still view/edit existing 60
* Cannot create new until under 50

**Recommendation:** Option 1 (grace period) - better UX

### Marketplace Listing Limit:

**Pro User with 1 listing:**
* ‚Üí "Create Listing" button disabled
* ‚Üí Tooltip: "Upgrade to Enterprise for unlimited listings or buy an add-on"
* ‚Üí Click: Modal with upgrade options

**Enterprise User:**
* ‚Üí No limits, create as many as needed

## üí° Pricing Psychology

### Why $39 for Pro?
* Below psychological $50 threshold
* Annual = $390 (saves $78, 16.7% discount)
* Positions as "affordable professional tool"

### Why $99 for Enterprise?
* 2.5x Pro price = clear value jump
* Still under $100/mo psychological barrier
* Annual = $990 (saves $198, 16.7% discount)

### Annual Discount Strategy:
* 2 months free (16.7% off) = strong incentive
* Upfront cash = better cash flow
* Higher LTV, lower churn (12-month commitment)

### Add-on Pricing Strategy:
* $5 increments (feels small)
* Forces upgrade decision when add-ons > $20/mo
* Example: User with +20 animals ($10) + +1 listing ($10) = $59/mo ‚Üí should just pay $99 for Enterprise

## Section 1: Why Professional Breeders Are Different

The breeder market differs from general pet owners in ways that matter for your business:

**Higher service frequency**
- Reproductive vets see the same breeder multiple times per year (progesterone testing, AI, ultrasounds, whelping support)
- Trainers work with entire litters, not individual animals
- Photographers shoot every litter for marketing images
- Transporters deliver animals to buyers across the country

**Higher service value**
- Breeders need volume pricing (10 puppies = 10x the service)
- Ongoing relationships mean repeat business
- Breeders refer other breeders (tight-knit community)

**Longer relationships**
- Breeders don't switch providers casually
- A good reproductive vet relationship lasts years
- Trust is earned once, then maintained

**Specific needs**
- Reproductive expertise (not just general practice)
- Breed-specific knowledge (structure, standards, genetics)
- Timing flexibility (breeding doesn't follow business hours)

General marketing (Google Ads, Facebook) reaches mostly pet owners. The BreederHQ Marketplace reaches professional breeders specifically.

## Section 4: How It Works

### Step 1: Create Your Listing

Provide:
- **Service type** — Which category best describes your service
- **Description** — What you offer and what makes you breeder-friendly
- **Species served** — Dogs, cats, horses, goats, rabbits, sheep (or all)
- **Geographic coverage** — Local, regional, national, or specific areas
- **Pricing approach** — Fixed rates, starting prices, or "contact for quote"
- **Credentials** — Licenses, certifications, breed club memberships
- **Portfolio** — Photos, testimonials, case studies

### Step 2: Get Verified (Optional but Recommended)

Upload documentation to earn a verification badge:
- License documentation
- Insurance certificates
- Professional certifications
- Breed club memberships

Verified providers receive more inquiries. The badge signals professionalism and accountability.

### Step 3: Receive Inquiries

When breeders search for your service type in your area:
- They see your listing with description and credentials
- They submit an inquiry through the platform
- You receive email notification + dashboard alert
- Inquiry includes their species, needs, and timeline

### Step 4: Respond and Convert

- Reply through the platform (response time is tracked)
- Discuss their needs and your services
- Convert inquiries to clients
- Build ongoing relationships

## FAQ Section

**Q: What does "first year free" mean exactly?**
A: You can create one service listing at no cost. The listing stays active for 12 months from creation. You receive unlimited inquiries during that time. After one year, you can upgrade to a paid tier or let the listing expire.

**Q: Are there any commissions on services I provide?**
A: No. BreederHQ charges listing fees only. We don't take a percentage of your service revenue. What you earn from clients is yours.

**Q: How do I get a verification badge?**
A: Upload relevant documentation (licenses, insurance, certifications) through your dashboard. Our team reviews submissions and awards badges to qualified providers.

**Q: What if I offer multiple services?**
A: The Free tier includes one listing. Premium tier allows up to 5 listings. Business tier allows up to 20 listings. Each service type can have its own listing with specific details.

**Q: Can I list in multiple geographic areas?**
A: Yes. You can specify your service area in each listing. Premium and Business tiers allow multiple listings, which can cover different regions.

**Q: How quickly should I respond to inquiries?**
A: Faster is better. Breeders notice response time, and prompt communication builds trust. There's no penalty for slow responses, but responsive providers earn better reputations.

**Q: What species do breeders on the platform work with?**
A: Dogs, cats, horses, goats, rabbits, and sheep. Dog breeders are currently the largest segment, but all species are represented.

**Q: How is this different from advertising on Facebook or Google?**
A: The BreederHQ Marketplace reaches professional breeders specifically—not random pet owners. Breeders searching here have ongoing service needs and higher lifetime value than typical pet owners.

**Q: I'm a breeder who also offers services. Do I need a separate listing?**
A: If you're a BreederHQ subscriber, service listings are included in your subscription. You don't need the service provider tiers.

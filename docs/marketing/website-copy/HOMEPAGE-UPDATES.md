# Homepage Update Recommendations

**Target Page**: `/` (index.astro)
**Status**: Recommendations - Pending Review
**Last Updated**: January 2026

---

## Executive Summary

The current homepage covers the basics but misses the platform's biggest differentiators. These recommendations add differentiation-focused messaging without requiring a full rewrite.

---

## Current Homepage Structure

1. Hero Section (Stop using spreadsheets / Start breeding smarter)
2. Species Support grid
3. Problem Statement (Scattered Data, Manual Processes, Poor Client Experience)
4. Species Bar (orange)
5. Features Overview (6 cards: Animal Profiles, Breeding Cycles, Waitlists, Genetics & Health, Client Portal, Business Tools)
6. Verification Badge explanation
7. CTA Section

---

## Recommended Changes

### Change 1: Update Features Overview Section

The current 6 feature cards are generic. Replace or augment with differentiation-focused messaging.

**Current cards:**
- Animal Profiles
- Breeding Cycles
- Waitlists & Placement
- Genetics & Health
- Client Portal
- Business Tools

**Recommended cards (replace or expand to 8):**

| Card | Headline | Description | Link |
|------|----------|-------------|------|
| 1 | **Breeding Intelligence** (NEW) | Stop guessing which pairings work best. AI-powered tools predict offspring outcomes, flag health risks, and recommend optimal matches. | /workflows/breeding-intelligence |
| 2 | Breeding Cycles | Track heat cycles, breedings, pregnancies, and whelping dates with automated reminders. | /workflows/breeding-cycles |
| 3 | Waitlists & Placement | Manage client applications, match families to animals, and communicate through every step. | /workflows/waitlists-and-placement |
| 4 | **Client Portal** (ENHANCED) | Give clients 24/7 access to updates, payments, contracts, and messaging—all in their own secure portal. | /workflows/client-portal |
| 5 | **Marketplace** (NEW) | Get discovered by serious buyers. List animals, earn verification badges, and manage inquiries in one place. | /marketplace |
| 6 | Genetics & Health | Store test results, track genetic lines, and make data-driven breeding decisions. | /workflows/genetics-and-health-testing |
| 7 | **Financial Tools** (NEW) | Track deposits, send invoices, record expenses, and see your program's financial health at a glance. | /workflows/financial-management (future page) |
| 8 | **Communications Hub** (NEW) | All your client messages in one inbox. Templates, document bundles, and response tracking included. | /workflows/communications (future page) |

**If keeping 6 cards, prioritize:** Breeding Intelligence, Client Portal (enhanced), Marketplace, Breeding Cycles, Waitlists & Placement, Genetics & Health.

### Change 3: Update the Verification Badge Section

The current section is good but could be stronger. It explains verification but doesn't mention the **4 badge types** or the **Quick Responder auto-badge** (which is unique and compelling).

**Current approach:** Single verification explanation paragraph.

**Recommended enhancement:** Replace single verification explanation with a visual grid of all 4 badges.

**Section headline:** Trust Badges That Mean Something

| Badge | Name | What It Means |
|-------|------|---------------|
| Shield icon | **Verified Breeder** | Identity and program documentation confirmed |
| Heart icon | **Health Testing** | Health testing documentation on file |
| Lightning icon | **Quick Responder** | Responds to inquiries within 4 hours (auto-earned) |
| Star icon | **Experienced Breeder** | 5+ successful placements completed |

**Supporting copy:**
> "Badges aren't bought—they're earned. Quick Responder and Experienced Breeder badges are awarded automatically based on actual behavior. No gaming the system."

### Change 4: Add Service Provider CTA

**Problem:** The homepage only speaks to breeders.

**Location:** After the main CTA section or in the footer area.

**Copy:**

> **Are you a service provider?**
> Veterinarians, trainers, photographers, and transporters: professional breeders need your services. List on the BreederHQ Marketplace and reach breeders who value expertise.
> [List Your Services →](/service-providers)

---

### Change 5: Update Hero Supporting Copy (Optional)

The current hero is good, but could hint at more:

**Current:**
> "BreederHQ is the all-in-one platform that gives professional breeders the tools they need to manage breeding cycles, waitlists, genetics, health testing, and client relationships—all in one place."

**Recommended:**
> "BreederHQ is the all-in-one platform for professional breeders. Manage breeding cycles, predict genetic outcomes, give clients their own portal, and get discovered on the marketplace—all in one place."

This hints at the three differentiators (genetics intelligence, client portal, marketplace) without adding length.
